Electromechanical diagrams
====

This folder contains the electromechanical schematics used in the construction of our PRO Future Engineers 2026 robot.

## Contents

### LEGO EV3 MINDSTORMS PROGRAMMABLE BRICK CONNECTIONS

This diagram shows the connections between the EV3 programmable brick and the various motors and sensors used by the robot, including the steering motor, drive motor, gyro sensor, and ultrasonic sensors.

### HUSKYLENS AI CAMERA CONNECTIONS

This diagram shows the electrical and communication connections required to integrate the HuskyLens AI camera with the EV3-based system, including the power supply used by the camera.
